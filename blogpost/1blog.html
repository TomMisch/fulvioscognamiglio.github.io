General info


This is the first post of my blog, which I believe is the most complicated, like when you shake hands for the first time and forget the other person's name. However, I would like to give a try (I don't have any other option I guess).
I don't want to annoy you with my bio (you can find it here). In this post, I want to give you a sense of the kind of stuff you will read here.
Mostly academic-related posts. I'm a scholar, what did you expect? Most of my time is spent researching, reading, writing, and teaching. Also, I feel this is part of who I am and not just a job from which to escape. I would like for this section to have two different aims. The first is to test ideas, theories, and analysis before thinking of transforming into a more concrete project. A sort of ongoing presentation (and indeed, I'll also implement a comment section). For the most part, you'll find posts about governance, wickedness, social innovation, and science of science (boring systematic literature review and bibliometric considerations).
The second aim is to keep a miscellanea diary, where I could talk about stuff I'm reading, considerations about the field, future projects or conferences. I do expect people to comment also on this other stuff ( and probably they would be the most popular posts, being the content more provocative for sure)
